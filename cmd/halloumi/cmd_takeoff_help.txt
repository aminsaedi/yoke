!yellow halloumi takeoff

!cyan Usage: 
  halloumi takeoff [flags] <release-name> <platter-path>

!cyan Examples:
  # deploy a local platter
  halloumi takeoff my-release ./main.wasm

  # deploy passing arguments to the platter
  halloumi takeoff my-release ./main.wasm -- -env prod

  # deploy a remote platter
  halloumi takeoff my-release https://github.com/my_org/infra/releases/v1.2.3-release.wasm

!cyan Flags:
